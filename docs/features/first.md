# Dosteon — First Contact Assessment

**This is not a test. It is a conversation on paper.**  
We want to understand who you are as an engineer before we go deeper.  
Be honest — a clear "I haven't done that" is more useful to us than a vague "I have some experience with that."

Short answers are fine. Examples are better than claims.

---

## 3. Experience — Tell Us About Specific Things You've Done

Answer only the ones that apply. Skip the rest — don't make things up.

**3.1** Have you built or worked on a user authentication system? What did it include — signup, login, password reset, email verification, sessions?

---

**3.2** Have you worked with a multi-tenant system (where multiple organisations or users share the same database but should only see their own data)? How was the data isolation handled?

---

**3.3** Have you written a database migration on a live production database? What was the change, and how did you make sure it didn't break anything?

---

**3.4** Have you integrated a third-party service (payment, email, storage, analytics, etc.)? What was it, and what was the hardest part?

---

**3.5** Have you fixed a security bug in a real system? What was it — briefly — and how did you find it?

## 4. How You Think — Before You Write a Single Line

**4.1** You are asked to build a "delete my account" feature. Before writing any code, what questions do you ask?

> *(We are not looking for the right answer — we are looking at whether you ask the right questions.)*

---

**4.2** You are about to deploy a backend change that touches the database schema on a production server with real users. Walk me through your process — step by step — from "code is ready" to "it is live."

---

**4.3** A user reports that after logging in they sometimes see a blank dashboard. You cannot reproduce it. What do you do?

---

## 6. Fit — Working With Us
